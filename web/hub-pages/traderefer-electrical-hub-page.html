Pricing Guide

How Much Does an Electrician Cost in Australia?

Electrical costs vary by job type, state, and urgency. Here's a general guide to help you budget — and TradeRefer helps you get real quotes to compare.

Job Type Typical Cost Range Notes
Standard call-out / hourly rate $80 – $150/hr Varies by state and tradie experience
Install power point or switch $150 – $280 Per point; more for complex locations
Ceiling fan installation $150 – $350 Higher if new wiring is required
LED downlight installation $60 – $130 per light Discounts common for larger quantities
Switchboard upgrade $1,500 – $3,500 Depends on size and safety switch count
Smoke alarm installation $60 – $150 per alarm Interconnected alarms cost more
EV charger installation $1,000 – $2,500 Depends on charger type and wiring run
After-hours emergency call-out $250 – $400+ flat fee Plus hourly rates on top

* Prices are indicative estimates only and vary by location, job complexity, and tradie. Use TradeRefer to get real quotes for your specific job.
